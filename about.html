ABOUT THE DECIDER

The DIY Decider was built to give you straight advice — whether that's the confidence to grab your tools or the good sense to put them down.

Tell the tool what you're trying to tackle. It'll size you up based on the task, the skill required, and what you tell it about yourself — then give you a verdict with zero sugarcoating.

Take the advice. Or don't. But don't say you weren't warned.

— Dave
How It Works
1
Enter your project — be specific. "Fix a leaky faucet" beats "plumbing stuff."
2
Answer 5 yes/no questions designed by a guy who's seen every DIY disaster imaginable.
3
Get a straight verdict — DIY or call a pro — plus a cost estimate and learning resources if you're going for it.
